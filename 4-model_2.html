Cosserat Rod
Kinematic Description
  • arc length of the reference configuration > $s$
  • Centerline > $\mathbf{x}(s)$
  • Material frame > $\{\mathbf{d}_3,\mathbf{d}_1,\mathbf{d}_2\}(s)$
  • Cross-section > $\mathcal{S}(s)$
  • Material point > $\mathbf{p}(s,X_1,X_2)$
Rod Motion
  • Translational velocity > $\mathbf{\dot{x}}(s,t)$
  • Angular velocity > $\mathbf{\omega}(s,t)$
  • Force strains > $\mathbf{\eta}(s,t)$
  • Moment strains > $\mathbf{\varkappa}(s,t)$
Kirchhoff Rod Assumptions
  • Slender
  • Shear center = Centroid
  • Small strains but finite displacements/rotations allowed
  • Negligible shear
  • Cross-sections are free to warp
  • Complete first order theory in $\alpha$ [Dill 1992]
Kirchhoff Deformations
Strains
  • Extension > $\mathbf{\epsilon}(s,t)$
  • Force strains > $\mathbf{\eta}(s,t)$
  • Moment strains > $\mathbf{\varkappa}(s,t)$
